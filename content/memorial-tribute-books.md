## Build the portrait together

No single relative holds every story. A sibling remembers childhood. A friend remembers the reckless years. A colleague knows the work that shaped them. A child knows the private rituals that made a house feel like home.

We gather those perspectives, along with photographs and the person's own writings, and shape them into one book that feels whole.

## Helpful materials

Letters, journals, recipes, military papers, favorite sayings, photographs, voicemail recordings, and stories shared at a celebration of life can all become part of the book.

For immediate help, see [how to write a eulogy](/blog/how-to-write-a-eulogy/) and [meaningful celebration of life ideas](/blog/meaningful-celebration-of-life-ideas/).
