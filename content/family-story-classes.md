## A manageable path from idea to keepsake

Most family-history projects stall because the person trying to do them has no clear order of operations. These classes break the work into small steps: choose a scope, prepare questions, record the conversation, collect photographs, organize the material, and decide what the finished keepsake should be.

Use the free [100 life story interview questions](/blog/100-life-story-interview-questions/) to begin, then bring a class to your library, church, club, or family group when you want more guidance.
