*Published July 8, 2026 · Updated July 8, 2026*

Before she was a Sports Illustrated cover model, Kate Upton was a champion horseback rider growing up in Melbourne, Florida. She moved to the Space Coast at age seven and spent her childhood in the show ring, winning national equestrian titles long before she was ever discovered. Here's the Melbourne story behind her rise.

The world knows Kate Upton from magazine covers. What it doesn't know is that she spent her Space Coast childhood covered in arena dust, in the saddle, chasing trophies at horse shows.

The model started as a Melbourne barn kid. Here's how.

## The short version

- **June 10, 1992** — Born in St. Joseph, Michigan
- **1999** — Moves to Melbourne, Florida at age 7
- **2000s** — Becomes a nationally ranked equestrian, winning American Paint Horse Association titles
- **2008** — Attends a modeling casting call in Miami and is signed the same day
- **2011** — Debuts in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue; named Rookie of the Year
- **2012, 2013, 2017** — Sports Illustrated Swimsuit cover model

## Three turning points

**Growing up in the show ring.** After moving to Melbourne as a girl, Upton threw herself into competitive horseback riding. She wasn't a casual rider. She competed at the national level with the American Paint Horse Association, earning multiple Reserve World Championships and a top-ranked youth standing. That was her childhood: discipline, early mornings, and the grit of competition, all on the Space Coast.

**The Miami casting call.** In 2008, as a teenager, Upton went to an Elite Model Management casting call in Miami. She was signed the same day. In a single afternoon, the Melbourne horse-show kid stepped onto a completely different path.

**Sports Illustrated in 2011.** Her debut in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue made her a household name almost overnight, and she was named the issue's Rookie of the Year. Cover after cover followed. The girl from the Brevard County barns had become one of the most recognized models in the world.

## How the Space Coast shaped her

Upton's Space Coast years weren't glamorous, and that's the point. She grew up doing something demanding and unglamorous: training and competing on horseback, learning to lose, learning to win, learning to perform under pressure in front of judges. That's a Melbourne childhood most people would never guess.

The poise and competitive drive that a modeling career demands didn't come from nowhere. They were built in Space Coast show rings, one event at a time, years before any camera found her.

## Frequently asked questions

**Where did Kate Upton grow up?**
Kate Upton was born in St. Joseph, Michigan, and moved to Melbourne, Florida, on the Space Coast, at age seven in 1999. She spent her childhood there and attended Holy Trinity Episcopal Academy.

**Was Kate Upton a competitive horseback rider?**
Yes. Before modeling, Upton was a nationally ranked equestrian who competed with the American Paint Horse Association and won multiple Reserve World Championship titles as a youth rider.

**How was Kate Upton discovered?**
