Florida's Space Coast has produced a surprising number of famous people, including rock legend Jim Morrison (born in Melbourne), surfing's greatest champion Kelly Slater (Cocoa Beach), comedian Carrot Top (Rockledge), and JoAnn Morgan, the only woman in the Apollo 11 firing room (Titusville). Here's a rundown of notable people with Brevard County roots, and the stories behind them.

We know the Space Coast for rockets. But this stretch of Brevard County has quietly launched more than spacecraft. It's the birthplace and boyhood home of rock stars, comedians, champions, and history-makers.

Here are some of the most famous, and a few you'd never guess. Each name links to the full story.

## Famous people from the Space Coast

1. **Jim Morrison** — The Doors' legendary frontman was born in Melbourne, Florida, in 1943, making one of rock's biggest icons a Space Coast baby. [Read his story.](/blog/jim-morrison-melbourne-florida/)

2. **Kelly Slater** — Widely called the greatest surfer of all time, Slater grew up in Cocoa Beach, learning on its small waves before winning 11 world titles. [Read his story.](/blog/kelly-slater-cocoa-beach/)

3. **JoAnn Morgan** — A Titusville girl who became the only woman in the Apollo 11 firing room at liftoff, and a barrier-breaking NASA engineer. [Read her story.](/blog/joann-morgan-apollo-11-firing-room/)

4. **Kate Upton** — Before the Sports Illustrated covers, Upton was a champion equestrian growing up in Melbourne. [Read her story.](/blog/kate-upton-melbourne-florida/)

5. **Carrot Top** — The red-haired prop comedian (real name Scott Thompson) was born in Rockledge and raised in Cocoa, the son of a NASA engineer. [Read his story.](/blog/carrot-top-rockledge-cocoa/)

6. **Daniel Tosh** — The Tosh.0 host grew up in Titusville, a minister's son who graduated from Astronaut High School. [Read his story.](/blog/daniel-tosh-titusville/)

7. **Doug Flutie** — The Heisman winner behind football's most famous Hail Mary spent his boyhood in Melbourne Beach, where his father worked in aerospace. [Read his story.](/blog/doug-flutie-melbourne-beach/)

8. **Darrell Hammond** — One of Saturday Night Live's longest-serving cast members was born and raised in Melbourne. [Read his story.](/blog/darrell-hammond-melbourne/)

## What all these lives have in common

Here's the thread that runs through every one of these stories, and it's the reason I write them.

Each of these famous people started as a local kid, in an ordinary Florida town, before anyone knew what they'd become. Jim Morrison was just a baby in a Melbourne hospital. Kelly Slater was a boy on a cheap board at Cocoa Beach. JoAnn Morgan was a teenager who took a summer job at the Cape.

The beginnings were ordinary. That's the point. Every remarkable life starts as a hometown story, and every hometown, including yours, is full of lives worth remembering, famous or not. The only difference between a story that lasts and one that's lost is whether somebody wrote it down.

## Frequently asked questions

**Who are some famous people from the Space Coast?**
Notable people from Florida's Space Coast include rock legend Jim Morrison (born in Melbourne), 11-time surfing world champion Kelly Slater (Cocoa Beach), comedian Carrot Top (Rockledge), Tosh.0 host Daniel Tosh (Titusville), model Kate Upton (Melbourne), SNL's Darrell Hammond (Melbourne), football's Doug Flutie (Melbourne Beach), and NASA trailblazer JoAnn Morgan (Titusville).

**Was Jim Morrison really born in Brevard County?**
Yes. Jim Morrison of The Doors was born in Melbourne, Florida, in Brevard County, in 1943. His family moved away while he was young, so his Space Coast connection is his birthplace.

**Which famous surfer is from the Space Coast?**
Kelly Slater, widely considered the greatest competitive surfer of all time, grew up in Cocoa Beach on the Space Coast and learned to surf on its local waves before winning a record 11 world championships.
