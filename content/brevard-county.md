## One service-area hub, not a stack of copied city pages

Every regional page below includes local context and the communities actually served. The writing is intentionally different because Melbourne, North Brevard, beachside, and Indian River County carry different histories and family experiences.

## What a personal historian does

We guide and record interviews, preserve the storyteller's voice, organize photographs and documents, write the chapters, design the pages, and prepare the finished book for printing.

Projects can begin as a short legacy letter, a veteran story, a memorial book, or a full family history. [Explore all services and pricing](/services/).
