## Personal history services across the Space Coast

Space Coast Stories is a Brevard County personal history studio. We help families record the memories that usually live only in conversation, then shape those interviews into a keepsake that can be read, shared, and passed down.

A project may be a short legacy letter, a full family history book, a veteran's service story, or a memorial book created with the people who knew someone best. The format changes, but the goal stays the same: preserve the details, voice, humor, and perspective that a family does not want to lose.

## Local interviews in Melbourne, Palm Bay, Viera, and beyond

In-person interviews are available throughout Brevard County, including:

- Melbourne and West Melbourne
- Palm Bay
- Viera and Suntree
- Rockledge and Cocoa
- Merritt Island and Cape Canaveral
- Titusville
- Satellite Beach, Indian Harbour Beach, and the beachside communities

Meeting locally makes it easier to sit with old photographs, walk through a family home, or include more than one relative in the conversation. When distance or health makes travel difficult, interviews can also happen by phone or video.

## What a personal historian does

A personal historian is part interviewer, part writer, and part careful organizer. You do not need a manuscript, an outline, or a perfect memory before beginning. We guide the conversation, record it, identify the strongest stories, and turn the material into clear chapters that still sound like the person telling them.

For book projects, we can also help select photographs, write captions, organize a timeline, design the pages, manage revisions, and prepare the finished book for printing.

## A good fit for many kinds of Brevard families

Some families begin because a parent is approaching a milestone birthday. Others want to preserve a grandparent's memories, document military service, collect the history of a longtime Space Coast family, or create something meaningful after a loss.

Brevard County has its own unusual history: citrus, military service, tourism, barrier-island life, the growth of Melbourne and Palm Bay, and generations connected to Kennedy Space Center and the space program. Those local details deserve to be kept alongside the larger story of a person's life.
