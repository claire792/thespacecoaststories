## The heart side of estate planning

A legal will explains what happens to property. A legacy letter explains what mattered to you.

Through one or two guided conversations, we help you share values, turning points, gratitude, apologies, blessings, and the lessons you hope your children or grandchildren remember. The result can be a polished letter, a small illustrated booklet, or the opening chapter of a larger legacy book.

## What you might include

- The principles that guided your life
- Stories behind the lessons you learned
- Messages for particular children or grandchildren
- Family traditions you hope continue
- Gratitude, forgiveness, and hopes for the future

You can read our guide to [legacy letters and ethical wills](/blog/legacy-letters-and-ethical-wills/) before deciding whether this smaller project fits.
