A free, relaxed hour of guided storytelling. Bring yourself, bring a friend, bring a parent. We'll hand out prompts and help you start capturing the stories worth keeping. No experience needed. Seating is limited, so come early.

## What to expect

The class uses specific, low-pressure prompts to help you identify the stories in your family that are most worth preserving. We will talk about how to ask follow-up questions, what to do when a relative says they have nothing interesting to share, and easy ways to save the conversation with a phone or notebook.

You are welcome to participate, listen, or bring a family member and try the prompts together. No writing or genealogy experience is needed, and nobody is required to share a private story with the room. The goal is to leave with a manageable first step and a short list of questions you can use at home.

Bring a notebook if you like, but all essential materials and prompts will be provided during the session.
