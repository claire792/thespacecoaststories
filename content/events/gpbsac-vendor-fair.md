Come find our table. We'll have the full lineup of Tell Me Your Story card kits and expansion packs, a sample legacy book you can flip through, and plenty of prompts to try on the spot. Stop by, pull a card, and tell us a story.

## What you can see at the table

You can handle the card decks, compare the different expansion packs, and flip through a completed sample book instead of trying to picture the products from a screen. Claire will also have examples of the kinds of interview questions that uncover more than names and dates.

This is a drop-in vendor fair, so there is no formal presentation or required start time. Come by for a quick question or stay long enough to try a prompt. Family members, caregivers, veterans, senior-community staff, and anyone curious about preserving a loved one's story are welcome.

Before you leave, you can take home a simple starting idea for your own family, whether that means one recorded conversation, a card kit for Sunday dinner, or a larger legacy-book project.
