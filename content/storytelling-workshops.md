## Programs built around real conversation

Topics can include family stories, funny memories, advice for younger generations, recipe stories, object stories, legacy letters, or the stories behind a lifetime of photographs.

Each session is structured enough to make participation easy and flexible enough to follow the room. Quieter participants can listen or write, and no one is put on the spot.
