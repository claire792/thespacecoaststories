## Stories rooted in this part of Florida

Barrier-island life has its own texture: hurricanes, surfing, fishing, beach schools, military families, old motels, and the way the ocean becomes part of a family’s identity.

A local interview can happen in a comfortable home, with family photographs and meaningful objects close at hand. That setting often brings out details that would never appear on a questionnaire.

Indialantic, Indian Harbour Beach, Satellite Beach, Melbourne Beach and nearby areas. Travel farther across Central Florida can be arranged for larger projects.
