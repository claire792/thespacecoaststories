## Stories rooted in this part of Florida

Growing families, longtime neighborhoods, military ties, local schools, small businesses, and generations connected to Harris, Northrop Grumman, healthcare, and the changing Space Coast all create stories worth recording.

A local interview can happen in a comfortable home, with family photographs and meaningful objects close at hand. That setting often brings out details that would never appear on a questionnaire.
